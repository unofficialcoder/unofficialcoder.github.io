PROJECT OVERVIEW

Ruby is a desktop voice assistant that uses speech recognition to perform tasks through audio instructions. It is designed to help users save time, be more productive, and be more accessible to people with disabilities. Ruby is written in Python and uses the Bardapi, Pyttsx3, Speechrecognition, and Webbrowser libraries.

Ruby can perform a variety of tasks, including:

  • Opening and closing applications
  • Searching the web
  • Checking the weather
  • Setting alarms
  • Playing music
  • Controlling the volume
  • Sending messages
  • And much more

Ruby is still under development, but it has the potential to revolutionize the way we interact with computers. By making it easier for people to control their computers with their voices, Ruby can help people save time, be more productive, and be more independent.

Here are some of the benefits of using Ruby:
  • Saves time: Ruby can help you save time by performing tasks for you with just your voice.
  • Increases productivity: Ruby can help you be more productive by freeing up your hands so you can focus on other tasks.
  • Improves accessibility: Ruby is accessible to people with disabilities, making it easier for them to interact with computers.
  • Provides entertainment: Ruby can be used for entertainment purposes, such as playing music or telling jokes.

If you are looking for a way to save time, be more productive, and improve your accessibility, then Ruby is a great option for you.
